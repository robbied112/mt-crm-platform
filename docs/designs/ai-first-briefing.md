## Vision

### 10x Check
CruFolio becomes your AI sales manager. The briefing is alive, it updates itself when new data arrives, tracks which actions you've completed, escalates risks you've ignored. It prepares your week ("Monday: call these 3 accounts. Tuesday: RNDC rep meeting, here's what to bring up"). It learns your patterns and connects to your CRM so completed actions become activity log entries and ignored risks become tasks. The static briefing evolves into a weekly conversation with the smartest sales analyst in the industry.

### Platonic Ideal
Open CruFolio Monday morning. One sentence: "Your Texas market is on fire. California needs attention. Here's your week." Below that, a conversation, not a dashboard. An AI that knows your business, remembers what you said last week, noticed your Colorado distributor hasn't moved product in 3 weeks. Charts exist as inline citations, small sparklines in the narrative text. Check off "Call Total Wine Pasadena" and CruFolio logs it in your CRM, sends a reminder, and next week asks "How did the call go?"

## Cost Estimate
- generateBriefing: ~80K tokens input + ~4K output = ~$0.30/briefing (Sonnet)
- askAboutData: ~60K tokens input + ~1K output = ~$0.20/question
- Territory-scoped: one briefing per unique territory per rebuild (not per user)
- Weekly digest: reuses latest briefing (no additional AI call)
- Monthly cost per tenant (solo user, weekly uploads): ~$1.20 briefings + $4 chat = ~$5.20
- Monthly cost per tenant (5 reps, 3 territories, weekly): ~$3.60 briefings + $20 chat = ~$23.60
